Conclusion¶

  • Power law transformation is applied on the test image and the image looks lighter when the value of gamma is < 1.
  • Image looks darker when the value of gamma is > 1.
  • Image thresholding is applied on the given test image:
    • For the threshold of 100 pixels with intensity of more than 100 are converted to white and remaining pixels are unchanged.
    • For the threshold of 150 is used to convert the given gray image to binary image.
  • Image negative operation is applied to the given test image which changes bright image to dark image.
